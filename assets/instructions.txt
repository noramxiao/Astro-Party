Space Wars is a a multiplayer space battle game where players control spaceships, 
aiming to outmaneuver and outgun their opponents in a variety of arenas.

HOW TO WIN: Win points by shooting your opponent. First to 5 points wins.

KEYBOARD CONTROLS
Player Red:
 - W: Rotate direction
 - Q: Shoot

Player Blue:
 - M: Rotate direction
 - N: Shoot

TIPS:
- Shoot at asteroids to destroy them.
- Double-click W/M for a boost.
